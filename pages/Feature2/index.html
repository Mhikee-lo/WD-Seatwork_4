Welcome To Honkai: Star Rail!

About

Honkai: Star Rail is a mobile game developed by miHoYo. It is a turn-based RPG game that features characters from the Honkai Impact series. The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world where players will be able to explore the world, battle enemies, and collect characters. The game features stunning visuals, engaging gameplay, and a compelling story that will keep players coming back for more.

Gameplay

Honkai: Star Rail follows the gameplay style of classic Japanese role-playing games: players build up a lineup of characters and control a team of up to four in turn-based combat.
